Telecom Italia aims to list tower unit before summer
LONDON Feb 20 Italian phone group Telecom Italia Chief Executive Marco Patuano said on Friday he expected to list a minority stake in its domestic mobile towers business before the summer.
Telecom Italia approved on Friday the transferral of 11,500 mobile phone towers to a company called Inwit of which it would keep a majority stake after the listing.
