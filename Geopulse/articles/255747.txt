TABLE-India's cenbank says reverse repo bids rise to 134&@&93 bln rupees
Sept 4 The Reserve Bank of India said on Friday it accepted all 32 bids for 134&@&93 billion rupees ($2&@&04 billion) at its one-day reverse repo auction on Thursday, through which it absorbs liquidity from the banking system.
