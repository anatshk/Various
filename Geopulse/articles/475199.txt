Russia's Gazprom Neft gets $350-mln credit line from Promsvyazbank
MOSCOW, June 4 Russia's Gazprom Neft, the oil arm of gas producer Gazprom, said on Thursday its board of directors had approved a credit line from Promsvyazbank (PSB) for up to $350 million.
The credit line will be available for three years, Gazprom Neft said in a statement.
