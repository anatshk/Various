US STOCKS SNAPSHOT-Wall St falls on rate hike, growth concerns
NEW YORK Nov 9 The S&P 500 index suffered its worst loss in six weeks on Monday as Wall Street braced for an interest-rate hike and fretted about weak Chinese trade data.
The Dow Jones industrial average fell 178&@&71 points, or 1 percent, to 17,731&@&62, the S&P 500 lost 20&@&53 points, or 0&@&98 percent, to 2,078&@&67 and the Nasdaq Composite  dropped 51&@&82 points, or 1&@&01 percent, to 5,095&@&30.
