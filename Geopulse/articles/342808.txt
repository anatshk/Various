Two Delaware judges toss generics lawsuits over jurisdiction
By Brendan Pierson
Two Delaware federal judges recently dismissed two separate patent cases against generic drug companies on jurisdictional grounds, highlighting the growing uncertainty surrounding jurisdiction in lawsuits concerning generic drug applications.
Both lawsuits were brought by brand-name drug companies alleging patent infringement against companies that applied to make generic versions of their drugs.
