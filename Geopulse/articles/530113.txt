ECB's Draghi to meet Greek deputy pm on Saturday -source
BRUSSELS, June 27 Greece's Deputy Prime Minister Yannis Dragasakis will meet European Central Bank President Mario Draghi on Saturday in Brussels to discuss the Greek debt crisis, a person familiar with the matter told Reuters.
The meeting is due to take place around lunchtime in Brussels.
