KCG to sell forex trading platform Hotspot to BATS Global
Financial services provider KCG Holdings Inc (KCG.N) said it will sell its foreign exchange trading platform, KCG Hotspot, to BATS Global Markets for $365 million in cash.
The transaction is expected to increase KCG's tangible book value by about $2 per share upon close.
The transaction is expected to be completed in the second quarter of 2015, KCG said on Wednesday.
