China to stop setting prices of most medicines from June 1
BEIJING May 5 China's government will stop setting the prices for most types of medicines from June 1 to improve the pricing of drugs in the world's most populous nation, the country's economic planner said on Tuesday.
The National Development and Reform Commission said in an online statement the change would encourage "reasonable" pricing of medicines.
