CORRECTED-MOVES-Private bank Pictet says Demole to step down as partner
(In MAY 11 story, corrects spelling of Pictet in headline and first paragraph)
ZURICH May 11 Jean-Franois Demole is stepping down as a partner at Swiss private bank Pictet et Cie, the Geneva-based wealth manager said on Monday.
Pictet's deputy chief executive for its asset management arm, Laurent Ramsey, will become a partner at the bank.
